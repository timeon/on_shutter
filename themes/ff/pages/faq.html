How does it work?

<%= configatron.site_name %> provides a channel for people to ask questions and share their answers and experiences. Tools are at your finger tip for voting on, commenting on, responding to, and bookmarking your favorite postings. The simple rule of "top voted comes first" allows the site to automatically stay relevant to the topics the community is focusing on.

What can I post here?

Any question on Christianity, the Bible and life situation handling, seeking advice and help through the Christian faith. Testimonies to share with others.

It started out from a bilingual community. You may post in either English or Chinese.

What should I not post here?

To prevent your postings from being flagged and possibly removed, and your account from being banned, avoid posting questions that are:

  • inappropriate, offensive or disrespectful in anyway
  • with content copyrighted to somebody else
